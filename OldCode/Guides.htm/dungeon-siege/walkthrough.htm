# Before you Start Notes
1 Choose a build and create your character. Commit to only 1 build per character.
# Act 1 Chapter 1: The Siege of Greilyn Beach Notes
2 Complete The Siege of Greilyn Beach
  1. Make sure all of your settings are correct.
  2. Increase the size of your minimap.
  3. Equip and use only weapons/spells that train your character build specialty.
  4. Don't save items or potions, for all your equipment will be lost at the end of the seige.
  5. Use your powers on the Ketril.

106 Destroy 2 barrels and open 1 chest before entering the underground sections.

114 Destroy 4 barrels and open 1 basket in the Hak'u camp to the northeast. Head north into the cave.

117 Enter the cave to the south. Open the chest.

119 Destroy 2 barrels and open 1 basket in the first Hak'u camp. Destroy 1 barrel and open 2 baskets in the second Hak'u camp.

# Act 1 Chapter 6: Leaving Greilyn Isle Notes
122 Head southwest. Pick up the Nettle Cluster by the cave entrance. Enter the cave and talk to Hrawn the Hak'u. Exit the cave and recite the Lesser Chant of Prosperity. Spam 1.
123 Continue on the path heading east. Destroy 1 barrel and open 2 baskets in the Hak'u camp. Open the tower Door. Destroy 3 barrels and open 1 basket inside it. Activate the Eastern Greilyn Jungle teleporter. Return to Eirulan.
124 Return to the Pet Shop to complete the Dire Wolf quest. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
125 Return to the Eastern Greilyn Jungle. Continue east on the path. Destroy 4 barrels and open 1 basket in the camp.
126 Continue across the bridge for now. Destroy 2 barrels in the Hak'u camp. Enter the cave.
127 Destroy 11 barrels and open 1 basket in the eastern portion of the cave. Destroy 6 barrels in the next cave nook. Destroy 2 barrels behind some stalagmites before the path goes up a ramp. Exit the cave.
128 Open the Door to the building. Get the chants, get the armor from the armor stand, and open 1 basket. Pick up the book "The Skath". Talk to Vix. He will join your party if you spam 1.
129 Enter the cave. Destroy 3 barrels by the sunlight pit. Go down the ramp and destroy 4 more barrels. Destroy 6 more barrels after you pass the big Hak'u camp. Destroy 5 barrels and open 1 chest in the next Hak'u camp. Destroy 2 more barrels. Destroy 5 more barrels in the next Hak'u camp and 3 more barrels in the center of this big room.
130 Head down through the small cave entrance to the west. Destroy 6 barrels, open 1 chest, pick up a Nettle Cluster, and open the Door. Return to the main cave.
131 Exit the cave. Activate the Kithraya Valley teleporter. Return to Eirulan.
132 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
133 Return to the Kithraya Valley. Destroy 3 barrels before going downhill. Destroy 4 barrels behind the mana bush. Enter the cave.
134 Destroy 7 egg sacks in the first side area. Destroy 3 more egg sacks after you go downhill. Destroy 2 more egg sacks in the room with the toxic gas.
135 Defeat the Plagued Maltratar Overlord to open a hidden area to the north. Destroy 18 egg sacks inside.
136 Head north after viewing the posession cutscene. Destroy 6 egg sacks. Destroy 3 egg sacks in the next room with toxic gas. Continue northeast out of the cave.
137 Destroy the Shard and pick up the Nettle Cluster. Do not recite a chant at the shrine. Talk to the Azunite Scholar. Use the portal. Activate the Azunite Desert Teleporter to the west. Spam 1.
138 Enter the camp and talk to Captain Suzor. Go inside the building and talk to Soldier Balamar. Use the Weapon Barrel and the Magic Bookcase. Spam 1.
138 Return to Eirulan. Give the 4 Nettle Clusters to Lumilla.
139 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
# Act 1 Chapter 7: Secret of the Azunite Desert Notes
140 Return to the Azunite Desert. Use the Portal to Greilyn Isle. Return on the path to the Kithraya Caverns. Recite the Lesser Chant of Prosperity at the incantation shrine. Once you enter the caverns, go south.
141 Destroy 26 eggscaks, open 3 chests, and defeat the Plagued Kithraya Hive Queen. Save and exit to return to Eirulan.
142 Take the lift to the Inn. Talk to Soldier Jordhan upstairs in the Inn. Take the lift to the Falls and talk to Tamari. Spam 1.
143 Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
144 Return to the Azunite Desert. Travel southwest up the ramp and defeat the enemies. Do not travel southeast yet, but instead go east from the teleporter through the open gate.
145 Continue north along the path, but do not talk to any of the Skath yet. Destroy 3 urns and open 1 chest. Speak to one of the Skath. Spam 1.
146 Open the west Door. Destroy 5 urns and open 3 chests in the Skath camp. Return to the first camp and open the north Door.
147 Open the tower Door and destroy 2 urns. Use the Lever, destroy 4 more urns, and open 1 chest. Push the Button near the western wall. Destroy 4 more runs and open 2 more chests in the opened room. talk to Master Thestrin. Return up via the Lever. Spam 1.
148 Continue up the path until the wide open area. Go south towards the next Skath camp. Destroy 3 urns and use 1 weapon barrel. Continue north and down the western stairs.
149 Destroy 7 urns and open 1 chest in the camp. Enter the northern cave.
150 Destroy 15 urns and open 3 chests in the cave. Open the door. Exit the cave.
151 Return up the stairs and enter the other cave. Open the door by the northern campsite. Go west to destroy 4 urns and open 1 chest. Exit the cave to the north to destroy 3 urns. Return into the cave and go east.
152 When you enter the shrine, destroy 5 urns and activate the bas relief on the southern wall to open a secret room with 3 urns, 1 armor stand, 1 weapon barrel, and 1 chant. Open the next 3 doors. Destroy 4 urns and open the next door. The southeastern chest is a powerful mimic. Open the other side chests and defeat the revealed enemies. Open the center chest last. Destroy 15 urns in the opened rooms and get the chant. Open the southern door.
153 Use the 2 levers to descend. Insert the stela into the sockets. Pick up the Silver Mirror and Map to the Lost Azunite Shrine.
# Act 1 Chapter 8: The Lost Azunite Artifact Notes
154 Exit through the western door and use the 2 levers. Exit to the surface and continue along the road. Destroy 4 urns and open 1 chest in the first ruins. Destroy 7 urns and open 1 chest in the next ruins. Open the door in the eastern tower for another chest and armor stand.
155 Activate the teleporter. Storage/Selling/Buying in Eirulan.
